### 5 Viral Ideas
1. **“AI Writes My Essay in 30 Seconds”**

2. **“Study Hacks 2024: Using AI to Ace Exams”**  
   Demonstrate how AI can create study guides, flashcards, or summaries from textbook content quickly.

3. **“Before vs After AI: My Homework Routine”**  
   A side-by-side comparison of writing an assignment manually vs using AI tools, highlighting time saved and improved ideas.

4. **“AI vs Human: Who Writes Better Thesis Statements?”**  
   Present AI-generated thesis statements vs student attempts, and invite viewers to vote or comment which is better.

5. **“Turn Your Notes Into Essays with AI - Here’s How”**  
   Show how students can simply input their class notes and get a cohesive essay or article draft.

### 3 Product Ideas
1. **AI Essay Draft Generator:** Tool that helps students get a full first draft by inputting essay prompts and some key points.

2. **AI Study Guide Creator:** An app or web tool that turns lecture notes or textbook chapters into summarized study guides or flashcards.

3. **AI Citation & Bibliography Helper:** A product that automatically generates citations and bibliographies in different formats (APA, MLA, Chicago) from student input.
