### 5 Viral Ideas

1. **“Untold Stories of History’s Greatest Leaders”**  
   Share lesser-known anecdotes about famous leaders (e.g., how a small failure led to a major success for Abraham Lincoln). Use engaging visuals and storytelling.

2. **“From Failure to Fortune: Historical Figures Who Overcame Odds”**  
   Showcase historical figures who faced massive failures but emerged victorious, linking their stories to modern-day motivation.

3. **“One-Minute History Lessons: Motivational Moments That Changed the World”**  
   Quick, punchy videos highlighting a single moment of inspiration or decision from history, perfect for Reels, TikTok, or Shorts.

4. **“History’s Biggest Risks That Paid Off”**  
   Stories about bold decisions (like the Wright brothers’ first flight or the moon landing) with motivational takeaways.

5. **“Then and Now: How Historical Innovations Influence Your Success Today”**  
   Create side-by-side comparisons of historical inventions or ideas and their modern impact in entrepreneurship and personal growth.
